About KaziConnect

Our Mission

KaziConnect aims to empower young people in Kenya by creating pathways to meaningful employment and entrepreneurship through technology. We bridge the gap between job seekers, employers, and training providers.

By providing a centralized system for employment matching and skills development, KaziConnect contributes directly to reducing youth unemployment and promoting sustainable livelihoods across Kenya.

The Problem We Solve

Youth unemployment remains one of the most pressing socio-economic challenges in Kenya. Approximately 13.4% of Kenyan youth are unemployed, with thousands of graduates joining the job market each year without sufficient access to opportunities.

Many educated and skilled youth remain unemployed not because of a lack of ability, but due to a mismatch between available opportunities and accessible information channels.
